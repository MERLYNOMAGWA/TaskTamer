About TaskTamer

TaskTamer was born during a gap year by Merlyn Omagwa, a 17-year-old student who was tired of boring planners and procrastination. Inspired by how games keep us hooked with points, rewards, and goals, she teamed up with friends to create a new kind of productivity appâ€”one that feels more like play than pressure.
